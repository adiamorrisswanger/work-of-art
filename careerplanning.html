Unit 1: Career Planning

Where do you want to be: artistically and professionally? Learn how to define your values, identify key choices, and develop a plan to achieve career goals.

Start Here

Dream Big

It's ok to have big dreams for your career. As the saying goes, "Shoot for the moon. Even if you miss, you'll land among the starts."

This first part of this unit will guide you through identifying your version of success.

Define Your Goals

The next part of this unit takes those big dreams and breaks them down into major goal categories.

Create Action Steps

This is where the rubber meets the road. Take those big goals and break them down into time bound action steps, creating your own map to success.

Take Action

The final part of this unit invites you to start - tomorrow - on the plan you just created.
